# Blitz Week: Your Fast Track to Launch

## What Is Blitz Week?

Blitz Week is an intensive, four-day engagement where you join Fluid's team at headquarters in Lehi, Utah. You work side-by-side with top executives, developers, and creative team members — the people who will actually build and run your platform — and by the end of the week, your customized storefront is fully deployed and live.

This isn't a demo or a planning session. When you walk out of Blitz Week, your platform is ready for business.

---

## Why Blitz Week?

**Speed to Market** — Your storefront is fully configured and operational by Thursday. Days, not months.

**Direct Access to Our Best** — You work alongside Fluid executives, senior developers, and creative specialists who can make decisions and build on the spot. No intermediaries. No back-and-forth after you leave.

**Real-Time Customization** — See something you want changed? It gets built right there. No tickets. No waiting. No follow-up calls.

**Executive Alignment** — With your leadership team present, every decision gets made together. You leave with complete buy-in and a platform configured exactly to your specifications.

**Production-Ready** — This isn't a prototype. Every setting, every feature, every customization is fully implemented and ready to go.

## How It Works

The week follows a comprehensive checklist covering every section of the platform — FairShare, Checkout, Mobile App, Website, Payments, and more. For each area, the team walks through the options, configures settings together, and confirms everything is right before moving on.

Developers and creative team members build in real time. When a customization, theme adjustment, or technical change is requested, it happens on the spot. Every result is reviewed and approved before moving forward.

Before arrival, Fluid's team completes an initial pass on the client's website — so from day one, the client can see their business is already understood. A strategy call is held ahead of time to align on expectations and technical considerations so the week hits the ground running.
