# App (Rep Tools)

## What It Is

Fluid's App is the rep-facing commerce and business management tool — available on both mobile and desktop — that gives distributors everything they need to share content, follow up with contacts, manage their business, and get paid, without switching between platforms.

The App is not a dashboard. It is not a reporting tool. It is a daily-use business companion designed for the way direct selling actually works: on the move, in conversation, driven by relationships.

---

## Naming and Product Structure

**Mobile App** — The mobile version of the rep experience. This is the primary tool most reps use in the field.

**Mobile App Builder** — The corporate-facing customization tool for designing the mobile app experience (what's shown, how it's organized, what features are available).

**App Builder / Rep Portal** — A newer web-based version of the rep experience, accessible via browser on desktop. It offers the same core features as the Mobile App. The App Builder is the corporate tool for designing this web portal experience. In the future, the App Builder is expected to replace the Mobile App Builder and manage both web and mobile experiences from a single tool.

For marketing purposes: "App" or "Mobile App" refers to the rep-facing tool. "App Builder" and "Mobile App Builder" refer to the corporate configuration tools.

## Core Rep Capabilities

### Share with Confidence
Every piece of content shared through the App — product links, pages, media, playlists — is automatically attributed through FairShare. Reps never have to wonder if credit will follow. Attribution is not something they manage. It is something the system handles.

### Replicated Site
Every rep has access to a FairShare-powered replica of the full company website. Every page, every product, every piece of content is automatically connected to that rep's attribution. When a customer arrives through a rep's replicated site link, all activity is attributed to that rep — across sessions, across devices, and even if the customer returns days later.

### MySite
The MySite is a rep-curated personal storefront within Fluid. Reps can highlight specific company content, products, and customized call-to-action buttons. The MySite is simpler and more focused than the replicated site — it's a hand-picked selection of what a rep wants to feature — displayed through the company's theme with FairShare attribution attached.

MySite is not a primary marketing focus. The replicated site and FairShare-attributed content sharing are stronger selling points for most audiences.

### Content and Media Sharing
Reps have access to a company-approved content library — videos, images, product pages, media assets — directly within the App. Every item is pre-attributed and ready to share with one tap. Reps do not need to search for assets in external folders or construct links manually.

### Playlists
Reps can create and share curated playlists — collections of products and media organized around a topic, a promotion, or a product category. Corporate can also create playlists for reps to use. Everything in a shared playlist carries FairShare attribution.

## CRM and Contact Management

### Contact Database
Reps maintain a contact database within the App, with grouping and segmentation capabilities. Contacts can be imported, organized, and tagged.

### Smart Links
A Smart Link is a contact-specific attribution link. Any activity that happens on a Smart Link is attributed to the specific contact it was generated for — name, phone number, email — regardless of who else receives or clicks the link. This is different from a general shared link, which attributes any click to the rep but not to a specific contact. Smart Links power the personalized follow-up workflows in the App.

### Activity Feed
Reps can see when a contact engages with content they've shared — when a page is viewed, when a video is started, when a product is explored. This turns follow-up from guesswork into a timely, informed action.

### Contact Interaction History
The full history of interactions with each contact is accessible within the App — messages sent, content shared, orders placed, and activity tracked.

## Catchups (AI-Powered Business Assistant)

Catchups is an AI-powered notification system built into the App that proactively surfaces business opportunities for reps — telling them when to reach out, what to follow up on, and what they may have missed.

All apps include the Catchups widget. Additional Catchup functionality is available to reps on the **Co-founder** (paid/pro) tier.

### Full List of Live Catchup Triggers

**Orders & Subscriptions**
- Cart Abandoned — contact left items in cart
- Upcoming Subscription — subscription processing in 3 days
- Subscriptions But No Enrollment — contact subscribed but hasn't enrolled
- Enrolled — contact created a new enrollment
- Multiple Orders But No Subscription — contact reorders repeatedly without subscribing
- Last Order Seven Days Ago — contact hasn't ordered recently
- Last Affiliate Order Ten Days Ago — affiliate's recent order activity
- Smart Link Share But No Order — Smart Link sent 2 days ago with no resulting order
- Recent Smart Link Share — contact recently received a Smart Link

**Activity**
- Recent Web Activity — contact viewed a shared page (daily summary)
- Video Viewed — contact started a video but didn't finish
- New Product — new product added, contact hasn't viewed or purchased it
- New Media Added — company added new media ready to share
- New Training Material — new training content available
- New Trending Media — media tagged as trending and ready to share
- New Reaction on Media — contact reacted to shared media
- Review on Product — contact wrote a review that can be shared

**Messages & Tasks**
- Overdue Task — rep's task is overdue
- Unresolved WebChat — web chat message unanswered
- Unresolved Popup Chat — popup chat message unresolved
- Unresolved Media Comment — contact commented on shared media without resolution
- Unread Message — unread company or contact messages (daily summary)
- Upcoming Event — company event starting within 2 days

**Groups**
- No Group Assigned — contact not assigned to any group
- Group Update Threshold Reached — contact's group hasn't been updated in 60+ days
- New Leader on Leaderboard — new affiliate has taken the top spot

**Other**
- New Purchase — affiliate makes their first purchase
- New Purchase of Lead — new lead purchase recorded
- Page Views — someone viewed a page the rep shared

Companies can also create **custom Catchup triggers** based on their own business logic, though this feature is not yet fully developed.

## Messaging

### Standard Messaging (All Users)
All reps have access to internal messaging within the App — direct messages to contacts, company broadcast channels, and team communications within the platform.

### Unified Messaging (Co-Founder Tier)
Reps on the Co-founder (pro) tier have access to Unified Messaging — a consolidated inbox that brings together SMS, email, and direct messages in one interface. This eliminates the need to manage multiple communication apps separately.

### Personal Business Phone Number (Co-Founder Tier)
Co-founder tier reps receive a dedicated business phone number, allowing them to communicate professionally without exposing their personal number to contacts and customers.

## Corporate Customization (App Builder / Mobile App Builder)

Corporate teams use the App Builder and Mobile App Builder to control what reps see and how the experience is organized. This includes:

- Drag-and-drop interface for building app pages and layouts
- Country, language, and rank-specific content variations — a new recruit in one market sees a different experience than a top leader in another
- Branded color schemes and visual assets
- Custom navigation and feature organization
- Pre-built templates optimized for distributor engagement and purchasing

Updates deployed through the builders go live across all devices immediately — no app store approval required.

---

## Desktop and Mobile, One System

The rep experience is available across both mobile and desktop without losing continuity. A rep can start on their phone, continue on their laptop, and complete a transaction without losing context. The system maintains state across devices.

The desktop experience (Rep Portal) offers more screen real estate for reps who manage higher volume, run reports, build content for their team, or prefer a full-screen workflow. It includes the same core features as the mobile app.

## Why It Matters

Reps don't wake up wanting to use a platform. They wake up wanting to build their business, help someone, and close a sale. The App exists to get out of the way and support that — not to add another tool to manage.

When the App works the way reps work — fast, in the field, in conversation — they use it. When they use it, they share. When they share, attribution is captured. When attribution is captured, trust builds. And when trust builds, the whole system accelerates.
