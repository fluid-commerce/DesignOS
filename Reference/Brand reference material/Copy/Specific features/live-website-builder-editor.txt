💡 What Is Live Editor?
Live Editor is a drag-and-drop website builder designed specifically for direct selling companies. It lets your internal team — not developers, not outside agencies — build, update, and manage pages in real time.
If you know how to use Microsoft Word or PowerPoint, you can use Live Editor. You don’t need to know code or design. It’s simple enough for anyone to use, but powerful enough to run your entire digital presence — rep pages, product launches, promotions, corporate updates, international rollouts, and more.
But Live Editor isn’t just another page builder. Because it’s part of the We-Commerce platform, everything’s already connected. That means your pages come with rep attribution (Fair Share), personalized content based on who’s viewing, fast checkout built in, and tools for internationalization — all without extra setup.
It gives you full control of your brand and messaging, without needing to rely on technical teams. So when the business needs to move fast, your site can keep up.
