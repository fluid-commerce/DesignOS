Live shopping isn’t new. What’s new is finally doing it the way direct selling actually works.

For decades, the most meaningful moments in direct selling have happened live — around kitchen counters, in living rooms, in test kitchens, and on video calls. People don’t buy because they were shown a price. They buy because they saw a product used, asked a question, and felt understood.

Live Shopping by Fluid brings that moment into the We-Commerce system — not as a separate tool, but as part of the full cycle. Demonstration, sharing, purchase, and attribution all happen in one place, without breaking trust or momentum.

This isn’t about turning commerce into entertainment. It’s about preserving the human connection that already exists in direct selling — and giving it modern infrastructure so it can scale.

When a product is demonstrated live on a We-Commerce site, it’s not just a video. It’s part of the transaction. The rep who shared it is recognized. The customer checks out instantly. And the system remembers how it all happened.

That’s live shopping, done the We-Commerce way.

What Makes Live Shopping by Fluid Different

Built into the We-Commerce cycle
Live shopping isn’t layered on top of e-commerce. It’s woven into the same system that already powers sharing, Fair Share attribution, compliance, and payments.

Designed for people, not influencers
Corporate teams can host polished demonstrations. Reps can host authentic, everyday streams. Both live together on product pages — because trust comes in many forms.

Attribution that just works
When a rep shares a live stream — corporate or personal — and a customer purchases, credit is automatically assigned, even if the purchase happens later.

Commerce without interruption
Customers can watch, ask questions, and check out in one place, with one-click payments and Buy Now, Pay Later options alongside the stream.

Compliance without friction
Real-time monitoring helps protect the brand and guide reps, without slowing down the experience or requiring constant oversight.

Live once. Work forever.
High-engagement moments are automatically turned into short clips and recordings that continue to live on product pages, long after the stream ends.

Discovery anywhere, ownership everywhere
Streams can be shared across social platforms, but transactions always happen on the brand’s own site — with full visibility and attribution intact.
