💡 What Is the Mobile App?
The We-Commerce Mobile App is a fully branded business tool your reps can carry in their pocket. It’s designed to give them everything they need to run their business — from ordering products and tracking subscriptions to following up with contacts and sharing content — all from a single place, on their phone.
For your company, it means more than convenience. It’s a way to increase rep engagement, activate your content, and drive sales — without needing to rely on multiple tools, training, or IT support.
All approved marketing materials — product pages, videos, PDFs, testimonials — are baked right into the app and instantly shareable. Reps don’t have to dig through SharePoint or remember special links. Every item is already personalized with their info and ready to send with one tap.
And because it’s part of the larger We-Commerce platform, everything is already connected: rep attribution, checkout, personalization, AI-powered follow-ups, and global compliance. It’s not just an app. It’s the front line of your digital strategy — built for scale, speed, and simplicity.
