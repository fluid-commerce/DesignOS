Benefits of Sync Tool
1. You’re no longer flying blind.
Right now, your data syncs live in hidden server folders, shell scripts, and some guy’s GitHub. If that person quits, you’re screwed. With Fluid, everything’s in a clean interface anyone can understand — no command line required.

2. You can finally see what’s working and what’s broken.
Today, you’re blind. If a sync fails, you don’t know why or where. Fluid gives you real-time visibility into every sync job — what ran, what failed, what changed — and alerts you if something breaks.

3. You only need one connection — not ten.
Instead of building a new integration every time you want to connect something to Exigo or ByDesign, you just connect once to Fluid. We normalize your data from any and all sources so it can be accessed by our open REST APIs.

4. It works with whatever weird setup you already have.
Your data structure isn’t “too custom.” We’ve seen it all. Fluid maps your current setup (Like Exigo Pillars or InfoTrax) — no matter how messy — and makes it usable. No rebuild required.

5. You can push and pull data in both directions — and control who wins.
You decide which system is the source of truth for each field. You can run one-way or two-way syncs, set rules, and choose when and how things update.

6. It’s fast — like, enterprise-grade fast.
We can sync hundreds of thousands of records per day without breaking a sweat. Users, products, orders, whatever you need.

7. It’s secure. Like, bank-level secure.
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We’re SOC 2 Type II compliant. No random file transfers. No sketchy SFTP folders.

8. You don’t need to start from scratch every time.
When you connect your commission engine to Fluid, we auto-fill about 80% of your Fluid setup. That means faster launches (within hours instead of months) and less grunt work.

9. You get a clean, always-updated backup in the cloud.
If your back office ever goes down, you still have a normalized, portable copy of your data — ready to move or restore.

10. You can finally kill all the old scripts and mystery servers.
No more custom sync files. No more worrying about someone’s pet project breaking. Just one clear system you control.
