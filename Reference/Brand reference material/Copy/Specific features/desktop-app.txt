💡 What Is the Desktop App?
The We-Commerce Desktop App is a fully branded business workspace designed to give your reps everything they need to run and grow their business — from a computer. It’s built for the reps who prefer a full-screen experience to manage orders, follow up with customers, view reports, and stay organized — especially leaders who are juggling more volume and responsibilities.
Just like the mobile app, it’s intuitive, modern, and loaded with the tools reps actually use — but optimized for multitasking and high-efficiency workflows. Whether they’re building pages for their team, managing subscriptions, reviewing performance, or sending follow-ups, it’s all in one place, always logged in, and ready to go.
From your side, the desktop app gives you total control over what reps see and how they see it. You can tailor dashboards and tools based on their role, region, or rank — without a developer. You can make updates and deploy changes in seconds. And because it's part of the We-Commerce ecosystem, everything ties together: rep attribution, fast checkout, content sharing, AI tools, and personalization — all already built in.
In short: it’s not just a nicer interface. It’s the rep’s home base — and your control center — for daily business activity.
