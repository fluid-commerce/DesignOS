# Audience Personas — Sales Enablement Agent Context

> Weight thresholds: 1-20 optional | 21-50 flexible | 51-80 strong preference | 81-100 brand-critical

## Overview (Weight: 85)

Target buyers span the entire C-suite. Each persona has different priorities, so messaging must be tailored. The Authenticity Advantage direction (see [messaging-frameworks.md](messaging-frameworks.md)) works across all personas but with different emphasis.

## CEO / Founder (Weight: 85)

**Primary concerns:** Growth, competitive advantage, company vision

**Lead with:**
- Category leadership — "We-commerce is the future, and you can lead it"
- Competitive FOMO — "The fastest-growing DS companies have figured this out"
- Strategic control — "Stop being held hostage by vendors"

**Authenticity Advantage angle:** Vision — "While others figure out how to make AI feel human, you already have real humans building real relationships." (Weight: 90)

**Sample message:**
"Your technology should be a competitive advantage, not a constant liability. Fluid is the operating system that lets you move as fast as your vision — launch in days, not quarters, while your competitors are still managing vendors."

**Best frameworks:** Category Creation, Transformation, Human-First AI
See [messaging-frameworks.md](messaging-frameworks.md) for framework details.

## CTO / IT Leader (Weight: 80)

**Primary concerns:** Architecture, integration, security, technical debt

**Lead with:**
- Unified architecture — "One platform eliminates integration maintenance"
- API-first design — "CLI tools, Cursor integration, developer-friendly"
- Vendor independence — "Token portability across payment providers"

**Authenticity Advantage angle:** Human-AI balance — AI amplifies reps rather than replacing human touchpoints. Technical buyers want to hear about AI capabilities framed as empowerment. (Weight: 85)

**Sample message:**
"Fluid replaces 15 point solutions with one unified platform — native payments, commissions, mobile, and themes that share the same data model. Third-party tokenization means you're never locked in. CLI tools let your team work in their IDE. Stop maintaining integrations and start building features."

**Best frameworks:** Pain-First, Speed & Agility, Ecosystem
See [messaging-frameworks.md](messaging-frameworks.md) for framework details.

## VP of Sales / Field Leader (Weight: 85)

**Primary concerns:** Rep retention, field enablement, trust, tools that help reps succeed

**Lead with:**
- Fair Share attribution — "Your reps will finally trust that they get credit"
- Mobile experience — "White-label app without IT involvement"
- One-click checkout — "Highest conversion = more commissions = happier reps"

**Authenticity Advantage angle:** The rep story — reps are real humans with real relationships, and that's the competitive edge AI can't replace. Fluid makes them superhuman. (Weight: 90)

**Sample message:**
"Your best reps are leaving because they don't trust the technology to track their work. Fluid's Fair Share algorithm proves attribution with full order journey visibility. Combine that with a white-label mobile app and one-click checkout, and your field will finally have tools they're proud to show prospects."

**Best frameworks:** Rep-Centric, Human-First AI, Pain-First
See [messaging-frameworks.md](messaging-frameworks.md) for framework details.

## COO / Operations (Weight: 80)

**Primary concerns:** Efficiency, cost, reliability, reducing operational complexity

**Lead with:**
- Vendor consolidation — "One contract replaces 15"
- Payment orchestration — "Optimize for cost and acceptance automatically"
- Self-service — "Marketing can change the site without IT tickets"

**Authenticity Advantage angle:** Trust = retention. Real relationships reduce churn. Operational efficiency compounds when the platform is unified. (Weight: 85)

**Sample message:**
"How many vendors do you manage right now? How many integration failures did you troubleshoot last quarter? Fluid consolidates your commerce stack into one platform with built-in payment orchestration, commission sync that stays up even when your engine is down, and self-service tools that let one person do the job of five."

**Best frameworks:** Pain-First, Speed & Agility, Ecosystem
See [messaging-frameworks.md](messaging-frameworks.md) for framework details.

## Cross-Persona Rules (Weight: 85)

**Always tailor the framework to the persona (Weight: 85):** Start with Pain-First discovery questions to understand their specific situation, then pivot to the framework that resonates with their stated concerns.

**The "WE" thread (Weight: 90):** The word "WE" should be intentional throughout — we-commerce, we build for you, we believe in people. This signals Fluid is about enabling human success. See [voice-rules.md](voice-rules.md) for voice principles.
