# Elevator Pitches — Campaign Agent Context

> Weight thresholds: 1-20 optional | 21-50 flexible | 51-80 strong preference | 81-100 brand-critical

## What Fluid IS (In One Breath) (Weight: 95)

**The only complete commerce platform purpose-built for direct sales.**

Not "we do everything" (too vague). Not "we-commerce" as the lead (requires explanation). The key words are COMPLETE (one platform, not 15), PURPOSE-BUILT (not Shopify + duct tape), and DIRECT SALES (you know exactly who it's for).

**The longer "what":**
"Everything Shopify gives you for e-commerce, plus everything direct sales actually needs — attribution, subscriptions, logged-in shopping, commission sync, mobile apps — in one connected platform."

- "In an age of AI slop, authenticity wins."

## 15-Second Pitch Options (Weight: 80)

For: Conference introductions, quick meetings, website subheadlines.

**Option A — Category Creation:**
"Fluid is the we-commerce platform — the complete operating system for direct sales companies. We combine Shopify-level checkout experience with every tool needed for attribution, subscriptions, and field enablement. One platform, no integration headaches."

**Option B — Pain-First:**
"Direct sales companies are drowning in point solutions that don't talk to each other. Fluid is the unified platform that replaces the chaos — commerce, payments, commissions, mobile, themes — all connected, all working. Finally."

**Option C — Competitive Displacement:**
"Shopify is great for guest checkout — but that's only 15% of direct sales revenue. Fluid is purpose-built for the other 85%: enrollments, subscriptions, logged-in shopping, attribution, and commission sync."

## 30-Second Pitch Options (Weight: 80)

For: Trade show conversations, discovery call openers, investor intros.

**Option A — Full Story Arc:**
"Direct sales companies are held hostage by their technology. They have 15 vendors, constant integration failures, and 15-click checkouts that kill conversion. Their reps don't trust that sales will be attributed correctly, so the best ones leave. Fluid is the we-commerce platform that changes this. We give you Shopify-level conversion with direct sales-native tools: Fair Share attribution, one-click checkout, payment orchestration, commission sync, mobile apps, and drag-and-drop themes. One platform where everything actually works together."

**Option B — Proof-Focused:**
"We've taken companies live in an afternoon. Not 90 days — an afternoon. Fluid is the we-commerce platform: a complete operating system for direct sales that includes commerce, payments, commissions, mobile, and themes. Companies come to us drowning in point solutions. They leave with a single platform where their field trusts attribution, their checkout actually converts, and their marketing team can make changes without calling IT."

**Option C — FOMO-Focused:**
"The direct sales companies winning right now have figured out that their tech is their competitive advantage. They're not fighting with Shopify plugins. They're not praying their payment provider talks to their commission engine. They're on we-commerce platforms purpose-built for relationship-driven selling. Fluid is that platform. While your competitors are launching campaigns in hours, are you still waiting 90 days for IT?"
