# Messaging Frameworks — Strategy Agent Context

> Weight thresholds: 1-20 optional | 21-50 flexible | 51-80 strong preference | 81-100 brand-critical

## The Core Challenge (Weight: 85)

Fluid faces a breadth vs. clarity paradox: the product's comprehensive nature is its greatest strength and biggest communication challenge. "We do everything" messaging fails — but the integration itself IS the product. (Weight: 85)

**Key insight:** Prospects can't remember 10 features, but they can remember 1 transformation. (Weight: 90)

## Recommended Primary Direction: The Authenticity Advantage (Weight: 95)

Framework H (Human-First AI) is the recommended campaign direction. It is timely (AI authenticity crisis is happening now), ownable (no one else in DS tech positions this way), reframes the category (DS goes from "old school MLM" to "the commerce model built for 2026"), works for all audiences, creates FOMO naturally, and is extendable into months of content.

See [voice-rules.md](voice-rules.md) for the full Authenticity Advantage thesis and "WE" philosophy.

## Framework A: Category Creation (Weight: 75)

**Core premise:** Don't compete. Redefine the game.
**When to use:** Website first impressions, establishing differentiation, forward-looking executive audiences.
**Sample pitch:** "We're building the operating system for we-commerce — where brands and sellers win together. While others force you to choose between Shopify simplicity and direct sales complexity, Fluid is the first platform built from day one for relationship-driven commerce."

## Framework B: Pain-First / Problem-Agitate-Solve (Weight: 80)

**Core premise:** Name their pain so specifically they feel understood.
**When to use:** Sales discovery calls, operations-focused buyers, prospects with active pain.
**Sample pitch:** "How many vendors do you manage? 10? 15? How often does something break between your payment provider and commission engine? Fluid is the unified platform that eliminates the integration chaos — one system, one contract, everything actually works together."

## Framework C: Rep-Centric / Field Success (Weight: 75)

**Core premise:** Happy reps = sticky reps = more sales.
**When to use:** VP Sales conversations, rep retention angles, field enablement pitches.
**Sample pitch:** "Your reps aren't loyal because your tech wasn't built for how they actually sell. Fluid is the platform that lets reps build their own business while you keep brand control. Better tools mean stickier reps. Stickier reps mean more sales."

## Framework D: Competitive Displacement (Weight: 70)

**Core premise:** Shopify is great for guest checkout. But that's only 15-25% of your revenue.
**When to use:** Prospects actively comparing Fluid to Shopify, head-to-head evaluations.
**Sample pitch:** "Shopify is the king of e-commerce. But guest checkout is only 25% of direct sales revenue. 75% comes from enrollments, subscriptions, and logged-in shopping — areas Shopify doesn't handle natively. Why hack it together when you can be on the platform purpose-built for direct sales?"

## Framework E: Speed & Agility (Weight: 70)

**Core premise:** What takes others 90 days takes us an afternoon.
**When to use:** Addressing migration fear, companies frustrated with slow IT cycles, Blitz Week proof.
**Sample pitch:** "Traditional direct sales platforms take 90-120 days to implement. We've launched companies in an afternoon. Fluid's drag-and-drop everything means one person can do the job of five."

## Framework F: Ecosystem / Extensibility (Weight: 65)

**Core premise:** Stop paying for every customization. Just click 'install.'
**When to use:** Customization-fatigued prospects, buyers familiar with Shopify App Store model.
**Sample pitch:** "Tired of paying for every customization? Fluid's Droplet Marketplace gives you Shopify App Store functionality for direct sales. One-click install. Best practices built by the community."

## Framework G: Transformation / Aspirational (Weight: 70)

**Core premise:** We don't fix what's broken. We unlock what's possible.
**When to use:** Brand-building, mission statements, emotionally resonant content, Shopify-style "dreamer" messaging.
**Sample pitch:** "To the founders building the next great direct sales company: we build for you. To everyone who believes commerce should be about relationships, not just transactions: welcome to we-commerce."

## Framework H: Human-First AI & Authenticity Advantage (Weight: 95)

**Core premise:** In an age of AI-generated everything, real human relationships are the ultimate competitive advantage.
**When to use:** Primary campaign direction. All audiences. Content marketing, LinkedIn ABM, thought leadership.
**Sample pitch:** "The internet is drowning in AI-generated reviews, bot followers, and synthetic content. But here's what consumers trust: recommendations from people they actually know. That's not nostalgia — that's the future. We-commerce is commerce built on real relationships, real experiences, and real trust."

## Layered Approach (Recommended Deployment) (Weight: 85)

Use multiple frameworks in sequence based on context and buyer stage:
- **Website (first impression):** Category Creation + quick proof points
- **Sales discovery:** Pain-First questions, then pivot to persona-relevant framework
- **Closing/negotiation:** FOMO messaging + Blitz Week proof + customer references
- **All touchpoints:** Human-First AI philosophy informs every framework's tone
