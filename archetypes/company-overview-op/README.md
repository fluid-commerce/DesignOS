# company-overview-op

## What It Is

A one-pager company overview layout for US Letter (612x792) print and digital distribution. Leads with a company positioning statement, three headline stats, an editorial narrative section, and a bulleted highlights list.

## Structural Pattern

Company headline and subheadline occupy the top zone, establishing the brand positioning. A three-column stat strip grounds the overview in hard numbers. The editorial body section provides a 2-3 paragraph qualitative narrative in flowing text. A bordered highlights box surfaces three key differentiators as scannable bullets. A minimal footer carries the CTA.

## Content Type Fit

- Company overview and capabilities documents
- Investor or partner introduction one-pagers
- Conference and event leave-behinds
- Sales outreach documents for new prospects

## When to Use

- The story is qualitative and narrative-driven (not outcome-driven like a case study)
- The company has 2-3 strong metrics worth leading with
- The audience needs to understand what the company is before understanding what it does
- Content will be printed or shared as a branded PDF document

## When NOT to Use

- The story is primarily a client transformation (use `case-study-op` instead)
- The content is product feature-focused (use `product-feature-op` instead)
- The audience already knows the company and needs specific deliverable info
- The post is for a social feed rather than a standalone document

## Components

- Company header: headline + subheadline
- Stat strip: 3-column grid of value + label pairs
- Editorial section: multi-paragraph flowing text body
- Highlights box: bordered list with 3 key differentiators, dot-prefixed via CSS
- Footer bar: CTA label anchored to bottom via `margin-top: auto`
