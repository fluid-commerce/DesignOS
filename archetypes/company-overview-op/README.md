# company-overview-op

## What it is

One-pager company overview layout: headline and sub-headline at top, a 3-stat strip, body copy, a bullet highlights section, and a footer CTA — fitting a complete company introduction on a single US Letter page.

## Structural pattern

The 612x792 canvas (US Letter) uses a vertical flex column. The top section holds a large company headline and a muted sub-headline describing the product or service. Below that, a 3-column stat strip displays key proof points (client count, retention rate, years in market). The editorial body section holds several paragraphs of descriptive copy. A bordered highlights box contains 3 bullet-point differentiators. A footer bar anchors the bottom with a CTA and divider line.

## Content type fit

- Company capability one-pagers for sales outreach
- Investor overview sheets
- Partnership introduction documents
- Product/service overview for conferences or meetings
- Leave-behind sales collateral

## When to use

- When a single-page document needs to convey company credibility, scale, and value prop
- For sales decks where each page represents a company overview
- When the content is: who we are + proof metrics + what we do + why choose us + CTA
- For printed or PDF-ready materials that need to feel polished

## When NOT to use

- When the content is a case study (use case-study-op)
- When you need to highlight a single product feature in depth (use product-feature-op)
- When the layout needs to be a social post, not a document (use LinkedIn or Instagram archetypes)
- When more than one page of content is needed

## Components

- Company header (headline + sub-headline)
- Stat strip (3-column grid, value + label per cell)
- Body text block (multi-paragraph editorial copy)
- Highlights list (3 bullet-point differentiators in a bordered box)
- Footer bar (CTA text, top border separator)
