The Challenge

Managing six separate vendors created compounding inefficiencies. Contracts overlapped, data lived in silos, and onboarding a new team member required six separate logins. Costs climbed 40% year over year with no corresponding productivity gain.

The Solution

Migrating to a unified platform gave every team member a single source of truth. Automated workflows replaced manual handoffs. Within 90 days, the company had decommissioned legacy tools and reduced monthly spend by nearly three quarters.
