# quote-testimonial-li

## What it is

LinkedIn landscape adaptation of the quote-testimonial pattern: a portrait photo anchors the left third of the frame, with the pull quote and attribution occupying the wider right panel.

## Structural pattern

A vertical split divides the 1200x627 canvas. The left zone is a full-height portrait container (400x627px) — taller and narrower than typical portrait crops, giving it a strong editorial presence. The right panel holds the italic quote text (36px) vertically centered, with the attribution name (bold, 26px) and title (muted, 20px) stacked below. The landscape format gives the quote panel generous width (688px), allowing longer quotes to breathe without compression.

## Content type fit

- Client testimonials and success stories
- Speaker highlight posts from events
- Interview excerpt posts
- Employee or founder spotlight posts
- Endorsement announcements

## When to use

- When a specific person's words are the content
- When you have a portrait photo of the speaker or subject
- For LinkedIn social proof posts (client results, endorsements)
- When name and title are critical credibility signals
- For conference speaker highlights

## When NOT to use

- When you don't have a portrait photo (use minimal-statement-li for text-only quotes)
- When the quote is very short and needs no visual weight (use minimal-statement-li)
- When the post is data-focused (use hero-stat-li or data-dashboard-li)
- When the subject is a brand, not a person

## Components

- Portrait container (left zone, full-height, object-fit cover)
- Quote text (italic, large, right panel)
- Attribution name (bold, below quote)
- Attribution title (muted, below name)
